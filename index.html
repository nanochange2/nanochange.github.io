Our product

Nanofarm

Our product, the Nanofarm, dispenses gold nanoparticles and methanotroph bacteria. Methanotroph attracts methane and forms formaldehyde. When gold nanoparticles interact with CO2, they convert it to CO, a more useful form of carbon. This is particularly useful for farmers and fossil fuel users who don't want to pollute and want a useful ingredient for bactericides, preservatives, natural gas, methanol, and functional polymers!

  • Eco-friendly
  • Eradicates 92% of greenhouse gases
  • Saves our government 4.3 billion dollars a year
  • Tackles all industries
